The Body as a Distributed System

A systems-thinking exploration of physiology through the lens of reliability engineering.

Status: MVP Scaffold

Project Overview

This project frames the human body as a distributed platform where organs are services, signals are events, and homeostasis is reliability. The goal is to make biological behavior easier to reason about using familiar software operations metaphors.
